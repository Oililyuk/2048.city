How to Play 2048

Play Game

What is 2048?

2048 is a single-player sliding block puzzle game. The game's objective is to slide numbered tiles on a grid to combine them to create a tile with the number 2048. However, you can keep playing after reaching 2048 to achieve higher scores!

How to Play

  1. Use the arrow keys (or swipe on mobile devices) to move all tiles in that direction.
  2. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one tile with the sum of their values.
  3. After each move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears in a random empty spot.
  4. The game ends when you can't make any more moves (the board is full and no tiles can be merged).

Tips & Strategies

  • Keep your highest number in a corner: This strategy helps maintain order and prevents the highest number from getting in the way.
  • Build a descending sequence: Arrange your tiles in descending order from the corner to make merging easier.
  • Don't spread out: Keep your tiles together to maximize merging opportunities.
  • Plan ahead: Think about where your next move will lead and how it will affect future moves.
  • Use the "snake" pattern: Try to arrange your tiles in a snake-like pattern from the corner.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I reach 2048?

Congratulations! You've won the game. However, you can continue playing to achieve a higher score. The game only ends when you can't make any more moves.

What's the highest possible tile?

Theoretically, there's no limit to how high you can go. The highest tile achieved by players is 131,072, but this is extremely rare. Most players aim for 2048 or 4096.

How is the score calculated?

Your score increases by the value of the new tile created when you merge two tiles. For example, merging two 4 tiles to make an 8 tile adds 8 points to your score.

What's the best strategy for beginners?

Start by keeping your highest number in a corner and build a descending sequence from there. Focus on maintaining order and avoiding spreading your tiles across the board.
